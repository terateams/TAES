A minimalist and professional logo design for "Allied AI", an AI education academy based in Tokyo.

Design Concept:
- The logo should feature a stylized monogram of "AAI" or "Allied AI".
- Incorporate "Tokyo Aesthetics": Clean lines, negative space (Ma), and a balance between tradition and futurism. Think Japanese modernism meets tech.
- The symbol should be recognizable even at small sizes (like a GitHub avatar or favicon).

Visual Elements:
- Geometric typography for "AAI", possibly interlocking or layered to suggest connection and neural networks.
- A subtle nod to Japanese calligraphy (Shodo) brush strokes integrated into the modern geometric letters, representing the blend of natural language and code.
- A clean, circular or squircle container (optional) to ensure it sits well on GitHub/Enterprise platforms.

Color Palette:
- "Indigo Blue" (Aizome) as the primary color: Deep, trustworthy, and traditional Japanese.
- "Sakura Pink" or "Electric White" as an accent color for the AI element, providing a sharp, modern contrast.
- High contrast against a white or very light grey background.

Text:
- Primary: "Allied AI" (Clean, sans-serif, modern).
- Secondary (Optional/Small): "アライドAI" (in a matching modern Japanese font, subtle, perhaps below or to the side, but the logo mark itself should stand alone without text).

Style:
- Flat design, vector art style.
- No complex gradients or shadows.
- Zen, precise, and intellectual.

Context:
- Suitable for a tech company profile picture, GitHub organization avatar, and favicon.

设计意图解析 (Design Intent)
GitHub 适配性:

强调 "High contrast" (高对比度) 和 "Recognizable at small sizes" (小尺寸可识别)，确保在 GitHub 头像（通常是圆形裁剪）中清晰可见。
推荐使用 "AAI" 字母组合 作为图形标（Logomark），因为全名在小图标中难以阅读。
文字处理 (Text):

"Allied AI" 作为主标题。
"アライドAI" 建议作为辅助元素或在横版 Logo 中出现，但在正方形图标（Avatar）中建议省略或极小化，以免显得拥挤。Prompt 中设置为 Optional。
东京美学 (Tokyo Aesthetics):

色彩: 选用 蓝染 (Aizome) 的深蓝作为主色，既代表科技（IBM Blue/Deep Tech），又是日本传统色，沉稳专业。点缀 樱色 或 电光白，体现 AI 的灵动与未来感。
构图: 强调 "Ma" (间) 的概念，即留白之美。不填满整个画面，保持呼吸感。
线条: 结合 几何线条 (现代/代码) 与 书法笔触 (传统/自然语言)，呼应 "Code in Natural Language" 的理念。
